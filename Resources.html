Resources

The computer science department in the University of Ghana offers various resources for students, graduates, workers and the general public who are interested in learning and developing their skills in the field of information technology.

Some of the resources are:

Undergraduate Programmes

The department offers two undergraduate degree programmes, Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Single-Major (3:2:1:1) and Major-Minor (3:2:2:1) as well as Bachelor of Science in Information Technology1. These programmes cover the core topics of computer science such as programming, data structures, algorithms, operating systems, databases, artificial intelligence, software engineering, etc. The programmes also provide opportunities for students to learn about emerging technologies and methods such as cloud computing, machine learning, image processing, etc. The admission requirements, course structure and curriculum can be found on the department’s website.

Graduate degree programmes

The department also offers graduate degree programmes such as Master of Philosophy (MPhil) and Master of Science (MSc) in Computer Science, as well as Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Computer Science. These programmes aim to provide students with advanced knowledge and skills in computer science, as well as to enable them to conduct original and high-quality research in various areas of computer science. The admission requirements, programme duration and research topics can be found on the department’s website.

Online short courses

The department offers online short courses in various I.T. fields such as web programming, information security and ethical hacking, digital marketing, database management, etc. These courses are open to senior high school graduates, tertiary students, graduates, workers and the general public who want to enhance their I.T. skills and knowledge. The courses are delivered through online platforms such as Zoom, Google Classroom, etc. The course fees, duration and registration details can be found on the university’s website.

Research and projects

The department is involved in various research and projects that aim to solve real-world problems and contribute to the development of the society and the economy. Some of the research areas include artificial intelligence, data science, computer vision, natural language processing, software engineering, etc. The department also collaborates with other departments, institutions and industries to conduct interdisciplinary and applied research. The department’s research output, publications and projects can be found on the department’s website.
